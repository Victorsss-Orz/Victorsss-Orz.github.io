About me!
{{ base_path }}
I am a second year Master of Science student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) in the Siebel School of Computing and Data Science. As a part of the Computing Education Research Area, my research interest lies in building educational tools to improve student learning, with autograding tools being the main focus. My current research involves building and evaluating an Automatic Short Answer Grading system to promote instant feedback in various open-ended response problems, and studying how and when the feedback can be helpful. I am also interested in understanding the perspectives of students, teaching assistants, and instructors on using potentially unreliable systems for assessment.
